Values represent our guiding principles: our broadest motivations, influencing the attitudes we hold and how we act.

Our values have been shown to influence our political persuasions, our career choices, our ecological footprints, the amount of resources we use, and for what purpose, and our feelings of personal wellbeing.

The Personal Values Questionnaire is a tool developed by social psychologists to assess peopleâ€™s values. It asks a series of questions intended to clarify the degree to which people prioritise different core values.

We offer this tool free on this website to help you better understand your values and see how they compare with others in Australia. Your answers are anonymous as we do not collect any identifiable information on survey participants.

At the end of the survey you will see your results and be able to compare them with the Australian average.

For more information on values and why they matter visit commoncause.org.au.
